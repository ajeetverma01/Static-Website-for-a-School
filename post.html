Annual Sports Day: A Celebration of Talent and Team Spirit

The Annual Sports Day at Om Sai International School was a grand success. Held on 10th January 2025, the event brought together students, teachers, and parents to celebrate physical fitness and healthy competition.

The day started with a vibrant march-past and oath-taking ceremony. Students participated in various events including races, relays, long jump, and tug-of-war. The event fostered teamwork, discipline, and sportsmanship.

Chief Guest Mr. Arvind Mishra encouraged students to actively participate in sports alongside academics. He emphasized the importance of a balanced lifestyle. Medals and certificates were awarded to winners across different categories.

Overall, it was a memorable day filled with cheers, enthusiasm, and proud moments. Om Sai International School continues to encourage holistic development through such engaging activities.
